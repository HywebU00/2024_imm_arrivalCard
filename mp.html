WHO SHOULD SUBMIT?

  • Foreigners without an ROC (Taiwan) Resident Certificate / a Diplomatic ID Card / a Resident Visa.
  • Mainland Chinese with a Multiple Entry & Exit Permit for the purpose of sightseeing.
  • Hong Kong and Macau Residents with a Single/Multiple Entry & Exit Permit, excluding those who enter Taiwan with an Online Entry Permit / Landing Entry Permit / ROC (Taiwan) Resident Certificate.
  • Nationals without Household Registration without an ROC (Taiwan) Resident Certificate.

WHAT DO I NEED TO SUBMIT?

  • Valid passport(s).
  • Traveler's valid email address.
  • Traveler's occupation and phone number.
  • Travel and accommodation information
